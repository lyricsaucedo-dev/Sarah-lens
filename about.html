About the photographer

Hi, I'm Sarah Michelle Maisano.

I didn't get into photography because everything in life felt perfect. I got into it because I started noticing how much beauty still exists even when things aren't.

I'm drawn to the in-between moments. The ones that aren't posed, filtered, or trying too hard. The quiet glances, the messy laughter, the raw, real emotion that you can't fake. That's what I want to capture for people. Something honest. Something that actually feels like you.

My style leans natural and a little moody, but still warm. I love depth, texture, and storytelling over perfection. I'm not here to make you look like someone else. I want you to see yourself in a way that feels genuine, maybe even a little more beautiful than you realized.

If you're someone who doesn't love being in front of the camera, you're kind of my favorite. I'll guide you, keep things relaxed, and make it feel way less awkward than you think it'll be.

At the end of the day, this isn't just about photos. It's about capturing something real enough that when you look back, you actually feel it again.

A few things I believe

  • ✦The best photographs are taken when no one is performing.
  • ✦Warm tones age better than trendy ones.
  • ✦A wedding day is a day, not a shot list.
  • ✦Print your photos. Hang them. Live with them.
