About the photographer

Hi, I'm Sarah Michelle Maisano.

I picked up my mother's old film camera when I was twelve and never quite put it down. Almost two decades later, I still feel the same small thrill every time I press the shutter — the quiet certainty that something tender just got to stay.

Sarah's Lens is my love letter to that feeling. I work mostly in natural light, shoot a mix of digital and 35mm film, and edit by hand to keep the warmth and grain that make a photograph feel like a memory rather than a screenshot.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me with a book in a sunny window, a cup of strong coffee, and my whippet Juno asleep on my feet.
